FAQ

Find quick answers to the most commonly asked questions about Vision X Cinemas.

Booking
How do I book tickets online?
Simply browse our movies, select a showtime, choose your seats on our interactive seat map, and complete your payment. You'll receive a booking confirmation with a unique booking number.
Can I choose my seats?
Yes! Our interactive seat map lets you pick your preferred seats. You can choose from Standard, Premium, IMAX, Dolby, and Deluxe options depending on the showtime.
Is there a reservation time limit?
Once you select seats, you have a 10-minute reservation window to complete your booking. If time runs out, the seats are released back for others to book.
Can I cancel or modify my booking?
Bookings can be modified or cancelled up to 2 hours before the show starts. Go to your profile page to manage your bookings or contact our support team.
Do I need an account to book?
Yes, you need to create a free account or log in to book tickets. This helps us manage your reservations and provide a personalized experience.
Payment
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Visa, Mastercard, credit/debit cards, and Fawry. You can choose your preferred method during checkout.
Is online payment secure?
Absolutely. All transactions are encrypted using industry-standard SSL. We never store your full card details on our servers.
How do refunds work?
Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days back to your original payment method. For Fawry payments, refunds are credited to your Fawry wallet.
Can I pay with Fawry?
Yes! Select Fawry at checkout and you'll receive a reference number. Pay at any Fawry outlet within your reservation window to confirm your booking.
Food & Drinks
Can I pre-order food with my ticket?
Yes! After selecting your seats, you'll have the option to add food and drinks to your order. This ensures your snacks are ready when you arrive.
Can I bring outside food?
Outside food and beverages are not permitted inside the theatre. We offer a wide variety of snacks, drinks, and combo deals at our concession stands.
Do you have vegetarian or allergy-friendly options?
We offer several vegetarian options. For specific allergies, please speak to our staff at the concession stand and they'll be happy to assist you.
General
What are your opening hours?
We're open Monday–Thursday from 10:00 AM to 12:00 AM, Friday–Saturday from 10:00 AM to 2:00 AM, and Sunday from 11:00 AM to 11:00 PM.
What experiences do you offer?
We offer Standard, Deluxe (premium recliner seating), IMAX (massive screen + laser projection), and Dolby Cinema (Dolby Atmos immersive sound) experiences.
Is there parking available?
Yes, free parking is available at all our locations with over 500 spaces. Valet parking is also available at select locations.
Do you have wheelchair accessibility?
All our theatres are fully wheelchair accessible with designated seating, accessible restrooms, and elevator access to all floors.

Still have questions?

Our support team is here to help. Don't hesitate to reach out!
