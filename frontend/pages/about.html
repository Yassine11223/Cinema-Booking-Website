About Us

Where stories come alive on the big screen. Discover the passion behind Vision X Cinemas.

Our Mission

At Vision X Cinemas, we believe that watching a movie should be more than just entertainment — it should be an experience. From the moment you walk through our doors, every detail is designed to transport you into the world of cinema.
Premium Experience
State-of-the-art IMAX & Dolby projection, immersive surround sound, and crystal-clear 4K laser displays.
Ultimate Comfort
Plush reclining seats, generous legroom, and luxurious Deluxe suites designed for ultimate relaxation.
Gourmet Snacks
Freshly prepared popcorn, artisan beverages, and a curated menu of cinema-inspired bites and combos.
Community First
We host special screenings, film festivals, and community events to bring movie lovers together.

Our Story

What started as a single screen in 2010 has grown into one of the region's most beloved cinema chains. Here are some milestones along the way.
2010
Vision X Cinemas opens its first flagship location with 4 standard screens.
2014
Expansion to 3 locations. Introduction of Deluxe premium seating.
2018
IMAX and Dolby Atmos theatres launched, setting a new standard in audiovisual quality.
2022
Surpassed 5 million guests. Launched online booking and the Scene Rewards program.
2026
Continuing to innovate with AI-powered recommendations and immersive 4DX experiences.
